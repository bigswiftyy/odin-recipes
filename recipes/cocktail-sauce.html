Cocktail Sauce

Description

A fun, fast and easy shrimp cocktail sauce recipe. A great sauce for your appetizer platter. Add a little kick!

Ingredients

  • Ketchup
  • Horseraddish Sauce
  • Chives
  • Fresh Minced Tomatoes
  • Pissman's Mustard

Steps

  1. Add a big old medium sized bowl of ketchup into your mixing bowl
  2. Next, add your horseraddish sauce
  3. Then we can add in our chives
  4. Finally, we add in our fresh tomatoes
  5. Now, mix the ingredients together with a folding motion
  6. Before we forget, add a little bit of Pissman's Mustard
  7. Get a vigorous stir going. Try to keep it chunky, like a salsa!
  8. Enjoy!
