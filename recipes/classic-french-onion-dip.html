Classic French Onion Dip

Description

A tasty classic french onion dip for your crispiest chips! This classic packs a flavorful punch!

Ingredients

  • Sour Cream
  • Minced Chives
  • Minced Onion
  • Onion Powder
  • Pissman's Mustard

Steps

  1. First, add the sour cream to your bowl
  2. Add a heaping handful of the minced onion
  3. Next, add in the chives
  4. Then, add a good two or three big pinches of your onion powder
  5. Now to add our secret ingredient: Pissman's Mustard. Don't be shy, get it in there!
  6. Finally, mix it all together with your wooden spoon. You really want to mix it well. It might get messy!
  7. Enjoy!
