Tim's Russian Dressing

Description

Tim's Russian Dressing is a combination of some of the finest sauces that money can buy! Exotic condiments like mayonnaise, ketchup and relish combine to make a tasty dressing that's good for your health.

This recipe is very simple, even your dog could make it! Well.. what are you waiting for? Make some dressing!

Ingredients

  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Relish

Steps

  1. First, add half a jar of mayonnaise to your bowl
  2. Next, add about the same amount of ketchup to the bowl. You want it to be a lot but not too much.
  3. Then, we can add a good, big spoonful of relish to the mixture.
  4. Finally, we can stir it all up really well so that our mixture is consistent. Get vigorous with it, dont be shy!
  5. Enjoy!
